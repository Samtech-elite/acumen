Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

Click on "Apply Now" to start your application. You'll need to upload your resume and writing samples. Our team reviews applications within 3-5 business days and will contact you for the next steps.

Previous writing experience is preferred but not required. We value passion, skill, and willingness to learn. Many of our successful writers started with minimal experience but demonstrated strong potential in their application samples.

Once accepted, you'll receive writing requests through our internal platform based on your skills and availability. You can choose which projects to accept, and our matching algorithm will suggest opportunities that align with your expertise.

We offer competitive rates based on project complexity and your experience level. Payments are processed bi-weekly through your preferred payment method (PayPal, direct deposit, or wire transfer). All payment details are clearly outlined before you accept any project.
